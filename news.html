Come along to one of our drop-in art workshops!

Guttersnipe presents: destruction/care, a micro-exhibition exploring rural youth frustration and community custodianship at Staveley bus shelter and loos.

14th August - 22nd September.

All workshops at Staveley Abbey bus shelter unless otherwise stated.

Tue 2nd July, 12pm - 3pm: lost dream posters
We are mapping and drawing our daydreams. How do they relate to the spaces around us?

Tue 9th July - 10:30am - 12pm: rose-tinted windows - at the Village Institute
We are looking at old postcards with photographs of Staveley through time, and tracing over them with our own memories.

Sun 21st July - 3pm - 5pm: are worms charming?
We are playing snakes and ladders on the concrete. How do you communicate with your environment?

Thu 25th July - 4pm - 7pm: word painting
We are matching landscapes to bold statements. We are making bold statements about our landscapes. Pick up a brush!
